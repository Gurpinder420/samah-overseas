How We Work

Our Proven 5-Step Process

A transparent, efficient, and candidate-centric process that ensures successful placements every time.

01
Requirement Analysis
We collaborate closely with employers to understand their exact workforce needs — job roles, skill requirements, timeline, compensation, and cultural fit expectations.
02
Candidate Selection
We tap into our vast talent pool and source candidates through job fairs, campus drives, and our database. Rigorous screening ensures only the best move forward.
03
Testing & Approval
Candidates undergo trade testing, medical fitness exams, and client interviews — in-person or via video conference. We facilitate employer approval at every stage.
04
Visa Processing
Our documentation team manages all visa applications, attestations, and emigration clearances — ensuring 100% compliance with both Indian and host-country regulations.
05
Deployment
Pre-departure briefings, ticketing, airport assistance, and post-arrival follow-up ensure a smooth transition. We stay connected throughout the contract period.
